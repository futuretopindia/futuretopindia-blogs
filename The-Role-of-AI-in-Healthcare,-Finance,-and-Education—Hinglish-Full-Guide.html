Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) ne technology ki duniya ko badal diya hai. Healthcare, Finance aur Education jaise sectors me AI ka use din-ba-din badh raha hai. Is guide me hum detail me dekhenge ki AI in sectors me kya role play kar raha hai.

AI Kya Hai?

AI yaani Artificial Intelligence ek aisi technology hai jo machines ko insaano ki tarah sochne aur seekhne ki ability deti hai. Iska main aim hai problems ko efficiently solve karna aur smart decisions lena.

Healthcare (Sehat) me AI ka Role

Healthcare industry me AI ne diagnosis, drug discovery aur robotic surgery jaise areas me revolution la diya hai.

  • Early Diagnosis: AI X-rays, MRI aur scans analyse karke diseases jaldi detect karta hai.
  • Drug Discovery: AI molecules ke combinations predict karke nayi dawaai develop karne me help karta hai.
  • Personalized Treatment: Har patient ke DNA aur health data ke basis par customized treatment.
  • Robotic Surgery: AI-assisted robots precision ke saath surgeries karte hain.

Finance (Paisa aur Banking) me AI ka Role

  • Fraud Detection: AI suspicious transactions detect karta hai.
  • Algorithmic Trading: Market data analyse karke smart trading decisions.
  • Credit Scoring: AI financial history ke basis par risk analyse karta hai.
  • Chatbots: 24/7 customer support provide karte hain.

Education (Padhai) me AI ka Role

  • Personalized Learning: Har student ki speed aur need ke hisaab se study plan.
  • Smart Content: Interactive books, quizzes, aur videos.
  • Virtual Tutors: 24/7 AI teachers jo doubts clear karte hain.
  • Teacher Support: Attendance aur grading automate karna.
