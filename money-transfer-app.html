üåé How a Money Transfer App Works

  1. User installs the app and completes verification (OTP/KYC)
  2. Links bank account or wallet or card to the app
  3. Initiates a transfer using mobile number, QR, UPI ID, or bank IFSC
  4. App routes the request securely through payment gateway or UPI interface
  5. Both sender and receiver get instant notification and logs

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Can I launch a UPI app without bank tie-up?

No. UPI apps need approval from NPCI and a PSP bank. However, you can build on top of aggregators like RazorpayX or Juspay who have bank partnerships.

Q2: Can I build a PayPal-like international money app in India?

Yes, but it requires RBI compliance. Use APIs from Wise, Stripe, or Payoneer for secure FX transfers.

Q3: Is KYC needed in money apps?

Yes. KYC (Aadhaar/PAN) is mandatory for wallets and large transactions as per RBI regulations.

Q4: Can I monetize my money transfer app?

Yes! You can earn through commissions, margins on exchange rates, subscriptions, or merchant fees.
